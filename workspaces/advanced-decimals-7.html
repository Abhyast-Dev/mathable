Fact Sheet: Decimal Measurement

Decimals help us write measurements smaller than one whole unit. Grams become kilograms by dividing by 1000.

1 kg = 1000 g. To convert grams to kilograms, divide by 1000.
250 g = 250 ÷ 1000 kg = 0.25 kg.
Decimal Place Value: 0.25 means 2 tenths and 5 hundredths.

Formula Hub

Grams to Kilograms

kg = grams ÷ 1000

250 g = 0.25 kg.

Kilograms to Grams

grams = kg × 1000

0.75 kg = 750 g.

Adding Decimals

Align decimal points.

Mixed Units

Convert to the same unit first.

Real-World Use

Cooking: Spice packets often show grams, but recipes may use kilograms.
Shopping: Vegetables may be weighed as 0.25 kg, 0.5 kg, or 1.25 kg.
Science: Small masses are easier to record using decimals of kilograms.
