Fact Sheet: Working with Fractions

Fractions help us describe parts of a whole, parts of a collection, and rates like distance per hour.

Repeated Addition: 3 × 1/4 = 1/4 + 1/4 + 1/4 = 3/4.
Fraction of a Quantity: 1/2 of 10 means 1/2 × 10 = 5.
Multiplying Fractions: Multiply numerators and multiply denominators, then simplify.

3 × 1/4= 3/4 km

Formula Hub

Whole Number × Fraction

n × a/b = (n × a)/b

Example: 3 × 1/4 = 3/4.

Fraction × Fraction

a/b × c/d = ac/bd

Example: 2/3 × 3/5 = 6/15 = 2/5.

Fraction of Quantity

a/b of n = a/b × n

Example: 3/4 of 20 = 15.

Simplify

Divide numerator and denominator by their common factor.

6/8 = 3/4.

Compare Fractions

Use common denominator or cross multiplication.

Unit Rate

Distance = rate × time.

1/4 km per hour for 3 hours = 3/4 km.
