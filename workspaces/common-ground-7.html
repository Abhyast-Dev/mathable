Fact Sheet: HCF and LCM

HCF means Highest Common Factor: the largest number that divides two or more numbers exactly.

LCM means Lowest Common Multiple: the smallest number that is a multiple of two or more numbers.

Use HCF when you want the biggest equal group, biggest square tile, or largest common size.
Use LCM when events repeat together, bells ring together, or cycles meet again.
Room Tile Example: For a 12 ft by 16 ft room, the largest square tile has side HCF(12,16) = 4 ft.

LCM

HCF × LCM Rule

For two numbers: product = HCF × LCM.

12 × 16 = 4 × 48.
