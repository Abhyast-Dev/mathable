Fact Sheet: What Area Measures

Area measures how much surface a region covers. We count how many unit squares fit inside a shape.

Rectangle: A 7 cm by 4 cm rectangle contains 7 Г— 4 = 28 unit squares, so its area is 28 cmВІ.
Triangle: A diagonal splits a rectangle into two congruent triangles. Each triangle has half the area of the rectangle.
Important idea: Perimeter is boundary length. It is not a measure of area. Two shapes can have the same perimeter but different areas.
